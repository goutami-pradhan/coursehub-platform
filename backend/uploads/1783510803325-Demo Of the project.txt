13. Expected Questions and Answers
Q: Why MongoDB?

Answer:

MongoDB is a NoSQL database that stores data in JSON-like documents. It is flexible, scalable, and integrates well with Node.js.

Q: Why JWT?

Answer:

JWT provides stateless authentication. The server does not need to maintain session data.

Q: Why bcrypt?

Answer:

bcrypt hashes passwords so that passwords are not stored in plain text.

Q: Difference between Authentication and Authorization?

Answer:

Authentication:

Who are you?

Authorization:

What are you allowed to do?
Q: Why Middleware?

Answer:

Middleware allows reusable logic such as authentication, logging, validation, and error handling.
